Solitude: The Imperial Seat

The seat of the High King. Visit the Bards College or witness an execution at the gates.

Essential Packing List

  • Fur Armor (for warmth)
  • Steel Sword or War Axe
  • Potions of Cure Disease (for Rockjoint)
  • Sweetrolls

Current State

Solitude is the stronghold of the Imperial Legion. As the capital of Skyrim, it remains firmly under Imperial control and serves as the military and political center of the war effort against the Stormcloak Rebellion.
