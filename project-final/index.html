Welcome, Traveler

The roads of Skyrim are treacherous, but the sights are worth the risk. From the golden plains of Whiterun to the icy docks of Windhelm, we provide the information you need to traverse the land of the Nords safely. Whether you are a merchant, a mercenary, or simply a wanderer, let us be your guide.

The Essential Holds

Whether you are a native who doesn't leave home, a traveler from High Rock, or a visitor from the south, these are the essential places to visit in the Province.

Trade Hub

The Central Hold

Home to the Companions and the Cloud District. A neutral ground for trade and commerce.

Jarl: Balgruuf The Greater
Imperial Capital

The Blue Palace

The seat of the High King. Visit the Bards College or witness an execution at the gates.

Jarl: Elisif the Fair
Thieves Den

The Canals

Known for the Black-Briar Meadery and the Ratway. Watch your pockets in the market.

Jarl: Laila Law-Giver
