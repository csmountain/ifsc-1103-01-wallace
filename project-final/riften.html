Riften: City of Thieves

Known for the Black-Briar Meadery and the Ratway. Watch your pockets in the market.

Essential Packing List

  • Fur Armor (for warmth)
  • Steel Sword or War Axe
  • Potions of Cure Disease (for Rockjoint)
  • Sweetrolls

Current State

Riften is controlled by the Stormcloak Rebellion. The city maintains a tense neutrality in commerce, but Stormcloak sympathizers run the government. The Thieves Guild operates freely under the radar.

Weather

Riften is warmer and wetter than other holds, but swamp fog and marsh hazards are common. Watch for disease-carrying insects and slippery terrain, especially near the docks and canals.
